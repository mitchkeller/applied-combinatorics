A linear extension of a poset \(\bfP=(X,P)\) is a total order \(L\) on \(X\) such that if \(x\leq y\) in \(P\text{,}\) then \(x\leq y\) in \(L\text{.}\) Give linear extension of the three posets shown in Figure 6.8.
