Label the sets in \(\mathcal{D}\) and \(\mathcal{U}\) respectively as \(D_1\text{,}\) \(D_2,\dots,D_d\) and \(U_1\text{,}\) \(U_2,\dots,U_d\) so that
\begin{equation*} \emptyset= D_1\subset D_2\subset D_3\subset\dots\subset D_d \quad\text{and} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} U_1\supset U_{2}\cdots \supset U_{d-2}\supset U_{d-1}\supset\dots\supset U_d =\emptyset. \end{equation*}
