Let \(n\) and \(k\) be positive integers with \(n\ge 2k\text{.}\) Then the maximum size of a family \(\cgF\) of subsets of \([n]\) subject to the restrictions that (1) \(A\cap B\neq\emptyset\) for all \(A,B\in\cgF\text{,}\) and (2) \(|A|=k\) for all \(A\in\cgF\text{,}\) is \(\binom{n-1}{k-1}\text{.}\)
