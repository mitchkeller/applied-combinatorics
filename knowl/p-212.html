Let \(x_1, x_2, \dots, x_r\) be nonzero real numbers with \(\sum_{i=1}^r x_i\neq 0\text{.}\) Then for every \(n\in \nni\text{,}\)
\begin{equation*} (x_1+x_2+\cdots + x_r)^n = \sum_{k_1+k_2+\cdots+k_r=n}\binom{n}{k_1,k_2,\dots,k_r} x_1^{k_1}x_2^{k_2}\cdots x_r^{k_r}. \end{equation*}
