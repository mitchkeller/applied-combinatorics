Let \(X\) be a random variable in a probability space \((S,P)\text{,}\) and let \(k>0\) be a positive real number. If the expectation \(E(X)\) of \(X\) is \(\mu\) and the standard deviation is \(\sigma_X\text{,}\) then
\begin{equation*} \prob(|X- E(X)|\le k\sigma_X)\ge 1-\frac{1}{k^2}. \end{equation*}
