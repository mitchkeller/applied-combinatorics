Consider the following advancement operator equation
\begin{equation} p(A)f=(A-r_1)(A-r_2)\dots(A-r_k)f=0.\tag{9.5.4} \end{equation}
with \(r_1,r_2,\dots,r_k\) distinct non-zero constants. Then every solution to (9.5.4) has the form
\begin{equation*} f(n)=c_1r_1^n+c_2 r_2^n+c_3r_3^n+\dots+c_kr_k^n. \end{equation*}
