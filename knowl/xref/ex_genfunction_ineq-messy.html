Exercise 27.

Consider the inequality
\begin{equation*} x_1+x_2+x_3+x_4\leq n \end{equation*}
where \(x_1,x_2,x_3,x_4,n\geq 0\) are all integers. Suppose also that \(x_2\geq 2\text{,}\) \(x_3\) is a multiple of \(4\text{,}\) and \(1\leq x_4\leq 3\text{.}\) Let \(c_n\) be the number of solutions of the inequality subject to these restrictions. Find the generating function for the sequence \(\{c_n\colon n\geq 0\}\) and use it to find a closed formula for \(c_n\text{.}\)
