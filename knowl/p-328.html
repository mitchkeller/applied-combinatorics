Let \(m,n\) be positive integers with \(m\gt n\) and let \(q\) and \(r\) be the unique integers for which
\begin{equation*} m = q\cdot n+r\quad\text{and} \quad 0 \le r \lt n. \end{equation*}
If \(r>0\text{,}\) then \(\gcd(m,n)=\gcd(n,r)\text{.}\) If \(r=0\text{,}\) then \(n\) divides \(m\text{,}\) and \(\gcd(m,n) = n\text{.}\)
