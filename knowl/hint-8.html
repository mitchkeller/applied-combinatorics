Hint 7.7.22.1.

  1. For a derangement \(\sigma\text{,}\) consider the integer \(k\) with \(\sigma(k)=1\text{.}\) Argue based on the number of choices for \(k\) and then whether \(\sigma(1)=k\) or not.
  2. You may find it easiest to prove this using the other recursive formula and mathematical induction.
