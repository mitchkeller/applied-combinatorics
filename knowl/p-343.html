Let \(n\) be a positive integer. Consider the following mathematical statements, each of which involve \(n\text{:}\)
  1. \(2n+7 = 13\text{.}\)
  2. \(3n-5=9\text{.}\)
  3. \(n^2-5n+9=3\text{.}\)
  4. \(8n-3 \lt 48\text{.}\)
  5. \(8n-3 > 0\text{.}\)
  6. \((n+3)(n+2) =n^2+5n+6\text{.}\)
  7. \(n^2 -6n + 13 \ge 0\text{.}\)
