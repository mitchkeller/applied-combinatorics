The dihedral group of the pentagon, \(D_{10}\text{,}\) contains \(10\) permutations. Let \(r_1=(12345)\) be the clockwise rotation by \(72^\circ\) and \(f_1=(1)(25)(34)\) be the flip about the line passing through \(1\) and perpendicular to the opposite side. Let \(r_2\text{,}\) \(r_3\text{,}\) and \(r_4\) be the other rotations in \(D_{10}\text{.}\) Denote the flip about the line passing through vertex \(i\) and perpendicular to the other side by \(f_i\text{,}\) \(1\leq i\leq 5\text{.}\) Write all \(10\) elements of \(D_{10}\) in cycle notation.
