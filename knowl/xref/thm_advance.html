Theorem 9.18.

Let \(k\) be a positive integer \(k\text{,}\) and let \(c_0,c_1,\dots,c_k\) be constants with \(c_0,c_k\neq 0\text{.}\) Then the set \(W\) of all solutions to the homogeneous linear equation
\begin{equation} (c_0A^{k}+ c_1A^{k-1} + c_2A^{k-2} + \dots+c_k)f = 0\tag{9.5.1} \end{equation}
is a \(k\)-dimensional subspace of \(V\text{.}\)
