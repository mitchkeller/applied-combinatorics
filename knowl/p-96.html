As an aside, we note that most calculators can’t add or multiply two \(20\) digits numbers, much less two numbers with more than \(500\) digits. But it is relatively straightforward to write a computer program that will do the job for us. Also, there are some powerful mathematical software tools available. Two very well known commercial examples are Maple® and Mathematica®. In this text, we will from time to time, make use of the open source computer algebra system SageMath 2 . We will sometimes embed interactive SageMath cells in the text, but you can also use SageMath for free online via CoCalc 3 .
