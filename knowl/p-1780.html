Let \(P\) be a regular \(n\times n\) transition matrix. Then there is a row vector \(W=(w_1, w_2,\dots,w_n\)) of positive real numbers summing to \(1\) so that as \(m\) tends to infinity, each row of \(P^m\) tends to \(W\text{.}\) Furthermore, \(WP=W\text{,}\) and for each \(i=1,2,\dots,n\text{,}\) the value \(w_i\) is the limiting probability of being in state \(S_i\text{.}\)
