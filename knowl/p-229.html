When all \(30\) students come in from recess together, they again form a single file line. However, this time the students are arranged so that the first student is from group \(1\text{,}\) the second from group \(2\text{,}\) the third from group \(3\text{,}\) and from there on, the students continue to alternate by group in this order. In how many ways can they line up to come in from recess?
