Suppose the ten books are labeled \(B_1, B_2,\dots, B_{10}\text{.}\) The sets of books given to the three children are pairwise disjoint and their union is \(\{B_1,B_2,\dots,B_{10}\}\text{.}\) How many different sets of the form \(\{S_1, S_2, S_3\}\) where \(S_1\text{,}\) \(S_2\) and \(S_3\) are pairwise disjoint and \(S_1\cup S_2\cup S_3=\{B_1,B_2,\dots,B_{10}\}\text{?}\)
