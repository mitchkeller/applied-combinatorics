Let \(V=(v_1,v_2,\dots,v_m)\) and \(W=(w_1,w_2,\dots,w_n)\) be partitions of an integer \(t\text{.}\) If \(V\) covers \(W\) in the poset \(\cgP(t)\text{,}\) then \(n\le m+1\) and there exist integers \(i\) and \(j\) with \(1\le i\lt j\le n\) so that the following statements hold.
  1. \(v_\alpha=w_\alpha\text{,}\) when \(1\le \alpha\lt i\text{.}\)
  2. \(v_\beta=w_\beta\text{,}\) when \(j\lt \beta\le m\text{.}\)
  3. \(v_i=1+w_i\text{.}\)
  4. Either (a) \(j\le m\) and \(w_j=1+v_j\text{,}\) or (b) \(j=n=m+1\) and \(w_j=1\text{.}\)
  5. If \(j>i+1\text{,}\) then \(w_\gamma=v_\gamma=v_i-1\) when \(i\lt \gamma\lt j\text{.}\)
